Incidence

Sat, 05 Jun 2021


Exactly a year ago, we went out, to the beach!
We went from afternoon to dawn!
I went there today. Guess what?!
I know that perfume now.
I passed by a gentleman who smelled just like you, well I dont know if you still smell that way, maybe you changed it because you changed me for another.
I ran back to him to ask him of his perfume.
Intriguing I guess?


I remember how we sat, on the shores.
I faced you sitting on your laps with my head laying in your chest.
I smelt you for hours, you refused to tell me what perfume it was because you said it wouldn't be beautiful to Me anymore.
You also said I could get it anytime and would mind to see you just to smell you cos I had your smell with me.


I got home at 3 am, got my dress off and smelled you till day break.
Today, I got back without your smell to smell but I know it now.
I want to buy the perfume, not to smell you, but for the memories!
