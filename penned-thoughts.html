Penned Thoughts

Thu, 17 Nov 2022


When Martin Luther said,
“If you want to change the world,
pick up your pen and write”,
He referred to me.


Lost emotions, Lost partners
Lost families, Lost friends, Lost words
But never my pen!
Even on a wall,
On the shores, On a floor, On a leaf


My pen writes,
It writes clearly,
It writes peacefully,
Breaks the heaped;
Anguish
Pain
Words


When I have a dream, my pen writes;
You’ve got to grab it and never let go!
Keep your face always toward the
sunshine and shadows
will fall behind you.


Your thought is your home,


The universe doesn’t give you
what you ask for with your thoughts,


It gives you what you demand
with your actions.


Your thought is your home?
A positive thought is an
extravagantly lavish home then.


Think it positive
Pen the thoughts on paper
Actualise the thoughts
with vehemence,
It becomes action.


Then, the universe will give you
what you demand with your actions.


Turn the unbeatable fears ,pain, doubts
and the seemingly implausible dreams
into words on paper and actualise them.


Life is either a daring adventure
or nothing at all!
